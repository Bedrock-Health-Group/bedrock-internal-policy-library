# Bedrock Internal Policy Library

This repository is the controlled internal policy library for Bedrock Health Group.

## Purpose

This repository stores internal policy infrastructure, templates, governance rules, and approved policy documents.

## Important

This repository is for internal Bedrock Health Group policy management. It is separate from public-facing policy indexes or external publication repositories.

## Core Principles

- No policy is considered approved unless it has gone through the required review and approval process.
- Policy content should be written, reviewed, and approved by appropriate business, compliance, and legal reviewers.
- The repository structure supports version control, document control, review workflow, and future publication through a policy portal.
