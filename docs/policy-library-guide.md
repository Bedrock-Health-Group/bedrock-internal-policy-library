# How to Use This Library

The Bedrock Health Group Governance & Compliance Policy Library is the controlled public policy library for Bedrock Health Group.

This library contains public-facing governance, privacy, security, and compliance policy records that have been prepared, reviewed, approved, and published through Bedrock Health Group's controlled GitHub review and publication workflow.

## Official Source of Record

The live policy page in this library is the official controlled record for public Bedrock Health Group policies published here.

Printed, downloaded, or PDF copies should be verified against the live policy library before being relied upon.

## Public vs. Internal Policies

This public policy library is intended for policies and governance records that may be shared publicly.

Internal-only policies, procedures, playbooks, administrative instructions, system details, investigation procedures, and sensitive operational materials should not be published in this public library.

Internal-only materials should be maintained in a separate private/internal policy environment.

## How Policies Are Published

Public policies are published through the following workflow:

1. A policy owner requests or drafts a policy.
2. The policy is classified as public or internal.
3. The policy is formatted using the Bedrock policy template.
4. The Policy Index and site navigation are updated when needed.
5. A GitHub pull request is opened.
6. Required reviewers review the policy and publication readiness.
7. The pull request is approved and merged.
8. GitHub Actions publishes the updated policy library.
9. The live policy URL becomes the controlled public record.

## Printed and PDF Copies

Printed or PDF copies generated from the live policy library are convenience copies. Users should verify the current version, status, and effective date against the live policy page before relying on a printed or downloaded copy.

## Questions or Corrections

Questions, corrections, or policy update requests should be submitted through the approved policy request or revision process.
