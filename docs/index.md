# Bedrock Health Group Internal Policy Library

This is the internal controlled policy library for Bedrock Health Group.

This library is intended to support structured policy management, document control, review workflow, version history, and future internal policy access.

## Library Purpose

The purpose of this library is to provide a centralized location for approved internal policies, templates, and policy governance materials.

## Current Status

This library structure is being established. No internal policy should be treated as approved or operational unless it has gone through the required review and approval process.

## Library Areas

- Compliance Program
- Healthcare Compliance
- Privacy & Security
- Governance
- Operations
- Finance & Administration
- Human Resources
- Vendor Management
- Training & Acknowledgements

## Document Control Principle

Policies should be drafted, reviewed, approved, published, revised, and retired through a controlled process.
