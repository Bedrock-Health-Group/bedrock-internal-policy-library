## 1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Bedrock Health Group uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on its websites, portals, and digital services.

Cookies help support website functionality, security, analytics, user experience, and service improvement.

## 2. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on a user’s device when visiting a website. Cookies may help a website recognize a user’s device, remember preferences, support secure sessions, or collect usage information.

Similar technologies may include pixels, tags, local storage, session storage, and analytics tools.

## 3. Types of Cookies We May Use

Bedrock Health Group may use the following types of cookies:

- **Strictly necessary cookies:** Required for core website functionality, security, navigation, and access to protected areas.
- **Performance and analytics cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with our website and services.
- **Functionality cookies:** Remember preferences and improve user experience.
- **Security cookies:** Help detect, prevent, and respond to security risks.
- **Third-party cookies:** May be used by service providers that support website hosting, analytics, embedded content, or digital tools.

## 4. Why We Use Cookies

We may use cookies and related technologies to:

- Operate and secure our website.
- Support login and session management.
- Understand website traffic and usage patterns.
- Improve performance and user experience.
- Maintain website reliability.
- Support compliance, security, and fraud-prevention activities.

## 5. Managing Cookies

Users may manage cookies through their browser settings. Most browsers allow users to block, delete, or limit cookies.

Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality, login features, or user experience.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Bedrock Health Group may use third-party services to support website functionality, analytics, hosting, security, or embedded content.

These third parties may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

## 7. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or organizational practices.

Updates will be posted with a revised Effective Date when applicable.
